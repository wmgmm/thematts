ü§ù Building AI Communities to foster responsible AI

No one knows everything about AI and that's exactly why we need each other.

That's the foundational insight behind Cardiff University's approach to AI adoption, one that's delivering measurable results and offering a blueprint for other organisations across Wales. Instead of pretending to have all the answers, we created a space for honest conversations about what's working, what isn't, and what we're learning along the way.

We're expanding our community to become part of a network of AI communities with people and external partners across Wales and further afield, sharing knowledge, solving real problems, and co-creating the future of AI for Wales.

Here's what we've discovered: it's all about the people. The organisations that thrive won't be the ones with the best AI tools - they'll be the ones with the best AI communities.

Partnership Opportunities

Join our growing network of organisations implementing the community approach:

  • Industry partners: Companies seeking practical AI adoption guidance and responsible implementation strategies.
  • Public sector organisations: Government bodies, councils, and public services exploring AI transformation.
  • Universities and education: Academic institutions building their own AI communities and research collaborations.

Why join our network? Connect with like-minded organisations, share best practices, access proven methodologies, and collaborate on solving real-world problems together. We believe that inclusive communities without boundaries are the most effective because diversity drives innovation.
