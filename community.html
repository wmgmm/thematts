No one knows everything about AI and that's exactly why we need each other.

This insight underpins Cardiff University's Community Approach to AI, a strategic Proof of Concept within the Digital Transformation Programme. Rather than positioning AI as a purely technical challenge, we're building collaborative communities that explore its opportunities, risks, and real-world applications.

AI raises complex questions from protecting critical skills and ensuring data security to addressing environmental impacts. Instead of claiming to have all the answers, we've created a space for honest conversations, safe experimentation, and shared learning.

We're now looking at expanding this community into a network of AI communities across Wales and beyond, connecting staff, students, and external partners to co-create a responsible, people-first future of AI.
