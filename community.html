No one knows everything about AI and that's exactly why we need each other. Because AI is actually about people.

This insight underpins Cardiff University's Community Approach to AI, a strategic approach within the Digital Transformation Programme. Rather than positioning AI as a purely technical challenge, we're building collaborative communities that explore its opportunities, risks and real-world applications.

AI raises complex questions from protecting critical skills and ensuring data security to addressing environmental impacts. Instead of claiming to have all the answers, we've created a space for conversations, safe experimentation and shared learning.

The universities that thrive won't have the best AI tools, but the best AI communities
