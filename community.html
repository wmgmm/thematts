The Community Approach

No one knows everything about AI and that's exactly why we need each other. This insight underpins Cardiff University's Community Approach to AI.

Rather than positioning AI as a purely technical challenge, we're building collaborative communities that explore its opportunities, risks and real-world applications.

"AI tools change fast. Communities last. The universities that thrive won't have the best AI tools, but the best AI communities."

Navigating Complexity

AI raises complex questions such as how to protect critical skills, keep our data safe and addressing environmental impacts.

To ensure Cardiff University responds to these appropriately we've created a space for conversations, safe experimentation and shared learning.

Our Pillars

Mindset

Cultivate curiosity and critical thinking. Move from binary thinking to nuanced evaluation. AI isn't perfect but could it be useful?

Conversations

Share what worked and what didn't. Create safe spaces for staff to admit confusion, seek help, and learn together.

Community

AI is actually about people, not just technology. Nobody has AI figured out yet. Shared learning means we all navigate this better.

Experiments

Learn by doing. Run small-scale pilots in safe-to-fail environments. Every experiment teaches something valuable.
