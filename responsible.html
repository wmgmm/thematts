Responsible AI Use

Guiding Principles for Individuals

Artificial Intelligence (AI) chatbots and tools represent an advancement in technology that can enhance our work processes and productivity. However, their effective use requires a thoughtful, people-centred approach.

This document outlines our personal principles for responsible AI adoption, focusing on keeping humans at the center of implementation. These are our perspectives on ethical and productive approaches.

Principle 01

Data Protection & Security

  • Only use IT-approved AI tools (e.g., Copilot with Work account).
  • Never input sensitive, confidential, or private data without approval.
  • Be cautious with intellectual property.
  • Consider security before sharing content.
  • When unsure, ask IT or your manager.
Principle 02

AI as Your Assistant

  • Enhance your work, don't replace judgment.
  • Start with small, non-critical experiments.
  • Focus on routine or repetitive tasks first.
  • Maintain human oversight on decisions.
  • View AI as a collaborative partner.
Principle 03

Choose Tasks Wisely

AI excels at:

  • Drafting initial documents.
  • Summarising meeting notes.
  • Basic research & info gathering.
  • Processing routine data.
  • Creating first drafts.
Principle 04

Keep Critical Tasks Human

  • Final decision-making.
  • Creative strategy & direction.
  • Complex problem-solving.
  • Relationship management.
  • Critical analysis & ethics.
Principle 05

Quality Control is Essential

  • Always verify AI outputs.
  • Cross-check facts and figures.
  • Review for context and tone.
  • Check for potential biases.
  • Track your edits and modifications.
Principle 06

Improve Productivity

  • Reduce digital waste & admin.
  • Automate repetitive responses.
  • Organise documents efficiently.
  • Make meetings more productive.
  • Focus on effectiveness first.
Principle 07

Professional Responsibility

  • Document your AI usage.
  • Share successes with colleagues.
  • Stay updated on capabilities.
  • Maintain your core skills.
  • Report concerns immediately.
Checklist

Before Using AI:

  • Is this an appropriate use?
  • Could this affect others negatively?
  • Am I maintaining standards?
  • Does this align with our values?
  • Have I checked the outputs?
