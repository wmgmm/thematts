A human-centric approach to AI adoption with The Matts. Helping teams find new ways of working, make better use of data, and free up time for the work that matters.

Driving AI success through Communities

AI capability sits with the people inside our public services, organisations and businesses. Build that capability with care and Wales gets the AI future we actually want.

That's the case for the AI Community approach. At Cardiff University / Prifysgol Caerdydd our AI Community has grown to over 1,000 members. People learn from each other, test ideas in safe spaces, and maintain the skills AI risks eroding.

Mindset

Cultivate curiosity and critical thinking. Move from binary thinking to nuanced evaluation. AI isn't perfect but could it be useful?

Conversations

Share what worked and what didn't. Create safe spaces for staff to admit confusion, seek help, and learn together.

Community

AI is actually about people, not just technology. Nobody has AI figured out yet. Shared learning means we all navigate this better.

Experiments

Learn by doing. Run small-scale pilots in safe-to-fail environments. Every experiment teaches something valuable and the best ones deliver real impact.
