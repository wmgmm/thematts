What Responsible AI Use Looks Like in Practice

Individual Level for the Workplace

Generative Artificial Intelligence (AI) chatbots and tools represent an advancement in technology that can enhance our work processes and productivity. However, their effective use requires a thoughtful, people-centred approach at both organisational and individual levels.

This document outlines principles for responsible AI adoption and usage in the workplace, focusing on keeping humans at the centre of AI implementation.

Guiding Principles for Responsible AI Use

1
Data Protection and Security
  • Only use approved AI tools for work tasks (check with IT if unsure)
  • Never input confidential information into unapproved AI tools
  • Check that data protection is active (e.g., look for the green shield in Microsoft Copilot)
  • If using personal AI tools, read the terms and conditions carefully
2
Think of AI as Your Assistant
  • Use AI to support your work, not replace your thinking
  • Start small with simple, low-risk tasks
  • Use AI for routine work that takes up your time
  • Always review and make the final decisions yourself
3
Choose Tasks Wisely
Good tasks for AI:
  • Writing first drafts
  • Summarising notes or documents
  • Finding information quickly
  • Formatting and organising content
4
Keep Critical Tasks for Humans
Tasks to keep for yourself:
  • Making important decisions
  • Building relationships with people
  • Solving complex problems
  • Creating strategy and direction
5
Quality Control is Essential
  • Check all AI outputs before using them
  • Verify facts and figures are correct
  • Make sure the content makes sense
  • Adjust the tone to suit your audience
6
Improve Productivity
  • Reduce time spent on repetitive tasks
  • Organise information more efficiently
  • Prepare for meetings faster
  • Focus on quality over speed
  • Give yourself time to learn and experiment
7
Professional Responsibility
  • Run small experiments to learn what works
  • Share both successes and failures with colleagues
  • Keep notes on how you use AI
  • Keep learning about how to use AI tools effectively
  • Continue developing your own skills
8
Before Using AI, Ask Yourself:
  • Is this the right task for AI?
  • Could this harm anyone?
  • Am I keeping professional standards?
  • Have I checked the output carefully?
