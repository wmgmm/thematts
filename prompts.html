Start Here: Work With AI, Not Just Prompt It

Use Copilot Like a Teammate
The real power of AI isn't in crafting perfect prompts it's about chating with Copilot. Share your thinking, give feedback, ask follow-up questions, and build ideas together through back-and-forth dialogue.
Why this matters: A natural conversation will get you better results than any single "perfect prompt." The back-and-forth unlocks deeper insights and helps you work through complex problems.
HOW TO HAVE A PRODUCTIVE CONVERSATION WITH AI: 1. START WITH CONTEXT (not commands): "I'm working on [describe situation]. Here's what I'm thinking: [your thoughts]. I'm stuck on [specific challenge]." 2. THEN ENGAGE IN DIALOGUE: - Build on responses: "That's interesting. What if we also considered [new angle]?" - Ask for reasoning: "Walk me through your thinking on this." - Share uncertainty: "I'm not sure about [aspect]. Help me think through different angles." - Push back: "I see your point, but what about [concern]?" 3. EXAMPLE CONVERSATION STARTER: "I need to improve our [process/system]. Here's the challenge: [describe tension or problem]. I've been thinking [your initial thoughts], but I'm wondering if I'm missing something. What factors should I consider?" Remember: Keep the conversation going. Don't expect perfect answers from a single message.
