Prompt Engineering Tasks

Task 1: Giving Direction - Using Role Prompting

Ask the AI to respond as different types of experts. This changes the tone and advice completely.

You are a counsellor. Reply to the statement "I'm feeling stressed".
You are a fitness trainer. Reply to the statement "I'm feeling stressed".
Task 2: Specifying Format Structure

Control how the AI presents information - tables, lists, step-by-step guides, etc.

Write a poem about studying for exams.
Write an SOP on the proper use of a knife and fork.
Return an Excel table about sunbathing with two columns (pros, cons) and fill in five examples for each.
Task 3: Evaluation - How Good is the AI's Output?

Get the AI to evaluate and rate its own suggestions.

Task: Create 5 new soft drink brand names For each name provide: Appeal & target market Potential issues (cultural or trademark) Ratings (1-10): Memorability, Distinctiveness and Global potential
Task 4: Prompt Chaining - Add All Methods Together

Combine role prompting, format control, and evaluation into one comprehensive prompt.

Task: Suggest appealing cat names. Role: Cat shelter volunteer helping families adopt. Tone: Cute and friendly, suitable for all ages. Constraint: Easy to call out, avoid human names. Format: Provide 10 names in a 3-column table: Name, Rating (1-10), Reasoning Examples: Whiskers, Luna, Paws
