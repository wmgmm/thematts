Productivity Exercises

Practical techniques to enhance workplace efficiency. Tap an exercise to begin.

Exercise 1: Explain it like I am 5 (ELI5)
Application: Perfect for learning new technical skills or simplifying complex concepts for stakeholders.
"Teach me [TOPIC] like I am 5 (ELI5)"
Exercise 2: Roleplay for Difficult Conversations
Application: Rehearse performance reviews, conflict resolution, or customer complaints in a safe environment.
"Roleplay: You're Mrs. Grudge, my grumpy neighbour knocking at 2 a.m. to complain about my noisy cats. I'm trying to calm you down. Stay in character and wait for my replies."
Workplace Examples: "You're an upset customer..." or "You're a team member missing deadlines..."
Exercise 3: Devil's Advocate
Application: Test proposals and identify blind spots before finalization.
"Act as a constructive devil's advocate. First identify potential weaknesses or blind spots, then suggest alternative perspectives. My scenario is: [scenario here]"

Follow-up:

"What important counterarguments am I missing?"
Exercise 4: Role Prompting
Application: Draft emails or documents from specific professional perspectives (e.g., Technical, Executive, Support).
"For a script: You are a medical professional. Reply to the statement 'My haemorrhoids are bothering me'."
"For a script: You are a comedian. Reply to the statement 'My haemorrhoids are bothering me'."
Exercise 5: Format & Structure
Application: Force AI to output specific formats like Tables, SOPs, SWOT analyses, or JSON.
"You run "Knights of the Round Table Pizza". Write a catchy jingle for your horse-drawn pizza delivery adverts."
"You run "Knights of the Round Table Pizza". Create an Excel table with three columns: Medieval pizza names, ingredients, and delivery times."
Exercise 6: Self-Evaluation
Application: Have the AI grade its own output to improve quality before you review it.
"I need a name for a new pensioner dating app. Provide five examples. Score each example on a 1-10 scale for Memorability, Distinctiveness and Global potential. Provide a reason for the score."
Exercise 7: Prompt Chaining
Application: Combine Role, Tone, Constraints, and Format into a single "Super Prompt" for complex tasks.
"Task: Suggest appealing fizzy drink names. Role: Young consumers (15-25). Tone: Humorous, but not childish. Constraint: Avoid existing UK product names. Format: Provide 10 names in a 3-column table: Name, Rating (1-10), Reasoning."
Usage Case: AI Meeting Notes
"Please summarize the following meeting transcript. Focus on the key points, decisions made, action items and any important deadlines... [INSERT TRANSCRIPT HERE]"
Usage Case: Excel Data Analysis
"Summarise this Excel file: PROVIDE: 1. OVERVIEW (rows/columns...) 2. KEY STATISTICS... 3. TOP INSIGHTS... Present in clear business language."
Usage Case: Text Categorisation
"Analyze the [Feedback] column. Add a new column named 'Category'. Classify each row into exactly one of these specific categories: 'Compensation', 'Management', 'Work Environment'... If unknown, label 'Other'."

Prompt Library

Curated prompts for rapid reuse.

Brainstorming & Ideas
Initial Idea Development
"Help me brainstorm some unique social media post ideas for [type of business]."
Report Structure
"Help me brainstorm some key sections for our [type of report]. Cover achievements, challenges, and plans."
Role-Based Communication
"You are a [professional role]. Reply to the statement '[insert question]'."
"You are a [role]. Help me draft an email explaining [topic] to [audience]."
Simplification (ELI5)
"Explain [topic] like I'm five."
"Explain [process] like I'm five. Then ask me questions to check my understanding."
Data Analysis
"Looking at this sales data, calculate the Marketing ROI %. Explain the formula step by step..."
"Analyse this dataset and identify: 3 unexpected findings, Critical areas for improvement, Key recommendations..."
Automation & Workflow
"I need a macro for my spreadsheet that calculates [calculation]. Include a chart... output a new Excel Macro-Enabled Workbook."
"Create 3 reusable email templates for [scenario]. Each template should have a slightly different tone..."
Content Summarization
"Please summarize the following meeting transcript. Focus on key points, decisions, action items... [INSERT TRANSCRIPT]"
"Convert this document into a comprehensive FAQ format... [INSERT DOCUMENT TEXT]"
Format Control
"Return a table about [topic] with [number] columns..."
"Take the Role of a [demographic]. What would be the name of a [product]... Provide examples in a column array..."
