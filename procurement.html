Exercise 1: Explain it Like I am 5 (ELI5)
Application: Quickly understand complex topics or prepare simple explanations.
"Explain [TOPIC] to me like I am 5 (ELI5)"

Replace [TOPIC] with: "framework agreements", "social value in procurement", "dynamic purchasing systems", etc.

Exercise 2: What Did I Miss? (AI as Your Teammate)
Application: Use AI to stress-test your plans before they fail.
"I am planning to [TASK]; tell me 3 things I've probably overlooked that will cause this to fail."

Replace [TASK] with your real scenario: "switch laboratory supplies provider", "tender for a new catering contract", etc.
